Privacy Policy

ImageMagick

As the organization responsible for maintaining and distributing the open‑source ImageMagick software, ImageMagick Studio LLC does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII). Likewise, the ImageMagick software itself is designed without mechanisms to gather PII from its users. Both the organization and the software distribution are committed to safeguarding user privacy by ensuring that no personal identifiers are collected during installation, configuration, or use.
